
 Service Provider / Business Profile Module
This section defines the Business Profile for service providers. This module allows businesses to showcase services, receive recommendations, and interact with clients.
The document includes required fields, tabs, actions, and user flow to ensure the service provider profile is fully interactive and usable by both clients and providers.
 Business Profile Overview
Each service provider will have a Business Profile separate from the Client Profile.
Business Profile Details:
Business name
Business logo / cover image
Primary service category (e.g., Cleaning, Painting, Handyman)
Secondary service categories (optional)
Service area / operating location
Short business description (About section)
Business website + email+ phone number
Any service Diploma+ certificate upload 
The document includes required fields, tabs, actions, and user flow to ensure the service provider profile is fully interactive and usable by both clients and providers.
The following tabs are included in business profile are :

Tabs in Service Provider Profile
Post Tab
Allows the provider to post updates or advertise services
Each post can include text, images, and optional pricing
Clients can view posts and comment or inquire about services
Recommendation Tab
Displays recommendations and feedback given by clients
Includes star rating (optional) and date of recommendation


Recommendations help build credibility and trust
Verify Badge Tab
Shows if the provider is verified by the app developer (AMK Decores)
Verification indicates reliability and professionalism
Only app admin can assign or remove the badge
Activity Tab
Lists all completed activities and jobs done by the provider
Includes details like date, service type, location (approximate), and client feedback
Where service provider can see all day activity 
Services Tab
Displays a detailed list of services offered
Each service can include a short description, category, and optional pricing or hourly rate
Clients can select and inquire about specific services directly from this tab
Availability Tab
Shows available days and time slots
Providers can mark emergency or same-day availability
Clients can request services based on available slots
Photos / Work Gallery Tab
Upload images of completed projects
Before & after photos to showcase work quality
Helps clients evaluate service standards
